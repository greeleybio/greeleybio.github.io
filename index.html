About our club

Greeley Biology Club’s mission is to provide opportunities to explore topics related to biology, some of which could be outside our regular curriculum, in fun and interesting ways.

All current and soon to be Greeley students are welcome to join. Your enthusiasm and curiosity is all that is needed!


A few things that we plan to do...

  • Share and create awareness among members regarding opportunities for research and summer options in biology related topics
  • Encourage participation in USA Biology Olympiad; share preparation tips
  • Invite and host guest speakers
  • Organize cool hands-on explorations (labs, etc)
  • Volunteer / raise funds for special events
  • ... your ideas are welcome!
